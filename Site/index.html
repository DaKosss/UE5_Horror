About the Game

Immerse yourself in the spine-chilling world of Survival Horror Game, where darkness is your greatest foe. Explore haunting environments, scavenge for light sources, and survive until the break of dawn. Beware of lurking enemies eager to extinguish your light and end your desperate struggle for survival.

Key Features

  • Intense Survival Gameplay
  • Dynamic Lighting Mechanics
  • Terrifying Enemies
  • Immersive Sound Design
